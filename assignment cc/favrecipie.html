Alfredo Pasta

Is an Italian pasta dish of fresh fettuccine tossed with butter and Parmesan As the cheese melts, it emulsifies the liquids to form a smooth and rich cheese sauce coating the pasta. The dish is named after Alfredo Pasta

Ingredients

  • spaghetti
  • 3 eggs
  • 100g Grated Parmesan cheese
  • 115g bacon
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1tbsp
  • 1/4 tbsp salt
  • 2 tbsp oil

METHOD

  1. Boil 1 packet of pasta in salted water. Cook spaghetti according to the instructions on the packet your pasta, leave 1 cup of pasta water and drain the rest
  2. Take a bowl and add the eggs, permesan cheese, black pepper and whisk all this together and set aside.
  3. In a large pan, heat oil over medium heat. Add bacon and cook until crispy.
  4. Add garlic to the pan and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  5. Reduce heat to low and add cooked spaghetti to the pan, tossing to coat with the bacon and garlic.
  6. Remove pan from heat. Quickly pour the egg and cheese mixture over the spaghetti, tossing continuously to combine. If the mixture is too thick, add reserved pasta water a little at a time until desired consistency is reached.
  7. Ready to serve, garnished with extra Parmesan cheese and black pepper if desired. Enjoy!
